# Minimalist Workout Plan

This is a 6-day split utilizing dumbbells, resistance bands, and bodyweight.

**Key rules:**
- Rest 60 seconds between sets
- Start light, focus on form first
- When 12 reps feels easy → increase dumbbell weight
- Every session = 40-50 minutes

---

### DAY 1 — UPPER A
* **Dumbbell floor press** — 4 sets x 10 reps
* **Dumbbell bent over row** — 4 sets x 10 reps
* **Dumbbell shoulder press** — 3 sets x 12 reps
* **Dumbbell bicep curl** — 3 sets x 12 reps
* **Dumbbell overhead tricep extension** — 3 sets x 12 reps
* **Band face pull (door anchor)** — 3 sets x 15 reps
* **Band lat pulldown** — 3 sets x 12 reps
* *(Optional)* **Dumbbell lateral raise** — 3 sets x 12 reps
* *(Optional)* **Push-ups** — 3 sets x 12 reps
* *(Optional)* **Chair dips** — 3 sets x 12 reps

**How to use this:**
- **Feeling good** → do all 10 exercises
- **Normal day** → do first 6-7, skip optionals
- **Tired or sore** → do first 6 only, stop there
- Never skip the non-optional ones — those are your core muscle builders
