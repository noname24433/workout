# Personalized Workout Plan

## Profile Overview
* **Weight:** 60 kg
* **Height:** 5'10"
* **Body Type:** Ectomorph / Hard Gainer

---

## 6-Day Workout Plan

### Day 1: Chest + Triceps
* **Push-ups** — 4 sets x 12 reps
* **Wide push-ups** — 3 sets x 12 reps
* **Diamond push-ups** — 3 sets x 10 reps
* **Chair dips** — 3 sets x 12 reps
* **Band tricep pushdown (door anchor)** — 3 sets x 12 reps

### Day 2: Back + Biceps
* **Band single arm row (door anchor)** — 4 sets x 12 each side
* **Band seated row (band around feet)** — 4 sets x 12 reps
* **Band lat pulldown (door anchor high)** — 3 sets x 12 reps
* **Band bicep curl** — 3 sets x 12 reps
* **Band hammer curl** — 3 sets x 12 reps

### Day 3: Legs
* **Bodyweight squat** — 4 sets x 20 reps
* **Lunges** — 4 sets x 12 reps (each leg)
* **Glute bridge** — 4 sets x 15 reps
* **Wall sit** — 3 sets x 40 seconds
* **Band lateral walk** — 3 sets x 20 steps (each side)
* **Calf raises (on a step or floor)** — 3 sets x 20 reps

### Day 4: Shoulders
* **Pike push-ups** — 4 sets x 12 reps
* **Band lateral raise** — 3 sets x 15 reps
* **Band face pull (door anchor)** — 3 sets x 15 reps
* **Band front raise** — 3 sets x 12 reps
* **Arm circles** — 2 sets x 30 seconds (each direction)

### Day 5: Core + Abs
* **Plank** — 4 sets x 45 seconds
* **Crunches** — 3 sets x 20 reps
* **Leg raises** — 3 sets x 15 reps
* **Bicycle crunches** — 3 sets x 20 reps
* **Mountain climbers** — 3 sets x 30 seconds
* **Superman hold** — 3 sets x 30 seconds
* **Band pallof press (door anchor)** — 3 sets x 12 reps (each side)

### Day 6: Full Body
* **Push-ups** — 3 sets x 12 reps
* **Band single arm row (door anchor)** — 3 sets x 10 each side
* **Squats** — 3 sets x 15 reps
* **Pike push-ups** — 3 sets x 10 reps
* **Glute bridge** — 3 sets x 15 reps
* **Plank** — 3 sets x 40 seconds

### Day 7: Rest Day
* Focus on active recovery, light stretching, and proper nutrition.
